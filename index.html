03 — Brand Understanding
What Le Havle Is

Before a single visual is created, I need to understand what Le Havle stands for. Based on our conversations, here is my understanding of the brand going into this project.

The Brand
Le Havle

A modern modest fashion brand for both men and women, backed by a Turkish textile manufacturer with deep expertise in fabric, printing, and quality. The brand is built on substance, premium materials from the factory floor to the final customer.

The Audience
Everyone Who Values Elegance

Muslim and non-Muslim customers who want fashionable, non-revealing clothing that does not compromise on style. Modern professionals, conscious dressers, and those who believe modest can also mean premium.

The Position
Modern. Modest. Premium.

Le Havle is not a religious brand. It is a fashion brand with values. Comparable references include Fresh Scarfs and Manuka, but Le Havle aims to step above that level, with a brand identity built to last and scale globally.

06 — Project Timeline
4 Weeks. 4 Phases.

Every phase ends with a meeting to review progress, collect feedback, and confirm direction before moving forward. Nothing moves to the next phase without your approval.

01
Week 1
Discovery & Strategy

Before any design starts, we go deep into understanding Le Havle. Who it is for, what it stands for, and how it should make people feel. This phase builds the strategic foundation that every visual decision will rest on.

  • Brand questionnaire and onboarding
  • Competitor and market research
  • Target audience definition
  • Brand personality and tone of voice
  • Brand story and mission writing
  • Mood board and visual direction
📞 End of Week Review Meeting
02
Week 2
Logo System, Typography & Color

With strategy confirmed, design begins. Two distinct logo concepts are developed for Le Havle. You review, give feedback, and we refine until one direction is selected and fully developed into a complete logo system.

  • 2 logo concepts presented for review
  • Color palette development
  • Typography system and font pairing
  • Logo refinement based on feedback
  • Final logo system in all variations
📞 Concept Presentation Meeting
03
Week 3

With the logo approved, the visual identity is extended across all digital touchpoints. Every template is built so your team can use them easily without breaking the brand consistency.
